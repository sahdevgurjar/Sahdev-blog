Unifest'24 - Galgotias University's Vintage Cultural Festival

Event Date: 06th April 2024

Galgotias Unifest 2k25

Galgotias University hosted the biggest cultural festival in Northern India, themed "Vintage" this year. The two-day extravaganza, held on April 5th and 6th, saw enthusiastic participation from various colleges across more than 20 events organized by the University's societies and clubs.

The highlight was the electrifying performance by renowned Bollywood playback singer Javed Ali, who enthralled the crowd with his mellifluous voice and popular hits like "Kun Faya Kun" and "Jashn-E-Bahaara".

Day 2 continued with more exciting events and concluded with a celebrity performance by Akhil Sachdeva, leaving the audience wanting more.
